THIS IS THE VIDEO TAG

Defination of video tag

The tag is used to embed video content in the HTML document

Syntax
Attributes of video tag 
          👍 width and height: specify the dimensions of the video player
          👍 controls: adds controls to the video player (e.g. play, pause, volume)
          👍 src: specifies the URL of the video file
          👍type: specifies the MIME type of the video file
