HTML Entities

HTML entities are used to display represent special characters and symbols that have the reserved meaning in HTML. These entities are espically important when you want to display characters that might conflict with HTML syntax or when you want to include characters that are not easily typed on a keyboard. that aren't directly available on your keyboard Below are some commonly used HTML entities: 

        &  - Ampersand (&)
        <  - Less than (<)
        >  - Greater than (>)
        " - Double Quote (")
        ' - Single Quote (')
        © - Copyright (©)
        ®  - Registered Trademark (®)
        € - Euro Sign (€)
        $ - dollar Sign ($)
        ™ - Trade Mark (™)nbsp;  Non-breaking Space
        ♥ - Heart Symbol (♥)
